PRIVACY POLICY

Bubble Harbor Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Bubble Harbor handles information when you use the game. Bubble Harbor is designed as a casual mobile puzzle game with offline core gameplay, local progress storage, and optional advertising services.

Information we may process

Bubble Harbor does not require you to create an account to play. The game may process limited device-related information needed to support app functionality, save local progress, protect the app environment, and deliver advertising.

  • Local game progress, unlocked levels, star ratings, and audio settings saved on your device
  • Basic diagnostics or crash information generated by the system or integrated SDKs
  • Advertising identifiers or similar device identifiers, where permitted by platform rules and your choices

Advertising and tracking transparency

Bubble Harbor may use third-party advertising SDKs to display ads. On supported iOS versions, the app presents Apple's App Tracking Transparency prompt before supported advertising SDKs initialize. If you deny permission, tracking for advertising purposes will remain disabled to the extent required by Apple and applicable SDK behavior.

How information is used

  • To save and restore your gameplay progress and preferences
  • To operate, secure, and improve the app experience
  • To serve and manage advertising content
  • To measure app stability and diagnose technical issues

Third-party services

The app may rely on third-party SDKs for advertising, attribution, analytics, security, or diagnostics. Those providers may process information according to their own privacy policies and platform obligations.

Data retention

Local progress and settings remain on your device until you remove the app or clear the app's data through your device settings. Third-party service providers may retain operational or diagnostic records for the time required by their systems and legal obligations.

Children's privacy

Bubble Harbor is not directed to children under the age at which parental consent is required in your jurisdiction. If you believe a child has provided personal information through the app inappropriately, please contact the publisher so the issue can be reviewed.

Your choices

  • You can manage tracking permissions through your iOS settings.
  • You can control music and sound effects directly in the game settings.
  • You can stop using the app at any time and remove it from your device.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect product changes, legal requirements, or service provider updates. The effective date at the top of this page will be revised when material changes are posted.

Contact

For support or privacy questions related to Bubble Harbor, please contact the publisher using the contact method provided on the app marketplace listing or support page.
